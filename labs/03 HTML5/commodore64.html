Commodore 64

History

Commodore 64, abbreviated C64, was introduced in 1982 and gained worldwide popularity. It is still the most sold computer in the world.

Current status

There's still a wide community of C64 enthusiasts. There are numerous C64 emulators, such as CCS64 (http://www.ccs64.com/) and Frodo (http://frodo.cebix.net/).
